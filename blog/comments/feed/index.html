]]> Comment on A tenoning jig for the Dewalt DW745 portable table saw by Julien Lecomte http://www.julienlecomte.net/blog/2011/03/574/comment-page-1/#comment-4630 Sat, 26 Nov 2011 05:01:34 +0000 http://www.julienlecomte.net/blog/?p=574#comment-4630 I actually sold my Dewalt DW745 table saw and bought the Bosch 4100 instead. My primary reasons for selling the Dewalt DW745 is because it cannot handle full size (4×8) sheets, and you won’t be able to use a dado blade with it. Otherwise, it will cut straight and true. Yes, the saw is very loud, louder than my Bosch table saw! However, the dust collection works better on the Dewalt than on the Bosch (I need to make some improvements to the Bosch saw to get the dust collection to work at its full potential) Hope this helps!
